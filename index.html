Our Story

Edmonton's Trusted Family HVAC Company

The Kowalski family has been keeping Edmonton homes warm in winter and cool in summer since 2008. What started as a one-truck operation has grown into a team of 12 licensed technicians serving thousands of homeowners across the greater Edmonton area.

We built this business on one principle: treat every customer's home like it's our own. That means honest assessments, fair pricing, and work we stand behind — every time.
